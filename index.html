Customs Declaration

Fun facts, personality & house rules

🗺️

Life goal: collect places, not things

The dream is to set foot in every Indian state — and honestly, every country too. Consider this your fair warning: I will be taking holidays.

⏰

9-to-6 means 9-to-6

Fully focused during working hours. Overtime is welcome — with pay. Unpaid overtime is a concept I have chosen not to believe in.

📵

Once I clock out, I'm off the grid

No work calls or messages after hours, please. That time belongs to my people, my plans, and me.

🌱

Give me a fair shot to learn

I ask questions because I actually want to get it right. Be patient, skip the scolding, and let the results speak once I've figured it out — they will.

🎈

Family & friends come first

I work to fund the trips and to be there for the people I love — every function, every "we need you," every celebration. Those days are non-negotiable and don't need an explanation.

💛

Delicate corporate girl energy

Kind words get the best out of me. I take feedback seriously — I just don't do well with scolding, and honestly, who does?
